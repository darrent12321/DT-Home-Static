About me

Well I wanted a menu and so created this About Me page!

After a few years in Desktop Support I finally have some time to learn some web technologies and thought it a good idea to share some of my journey. Starting with this basic blog I will be running through different lessons from various sources and as I build on my knowledge I build up my portfolio.

This blog doesn't reflect everything I have learned as many lessons do not end up with any sort of artifact that can be presented here, CodingGame.com being a prime example.

Sources will include YouTube, books and various websites. If I can remember I will give credit where it is due.

A big thank you to all those that put their own time in to create lessons and other content for everyone. It's very much appreciated.

Please bear in mind that some of the examples will be half baked rubbish, but as long as I learn a lesson at least I will be happy!
